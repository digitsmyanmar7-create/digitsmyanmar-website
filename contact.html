Contact FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What's the best way to contact Digits Myanmar?

For general inquiries, email is usually the fastest way to reach us. For urgent matters, you can call our main office. If you need technical support, please use our dedicated support channels.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For emails received on weekends or holidays, we'll respond on the next business day.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

Yes, you can visit our offices during business hours. However, for meetings with specific team members, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure they're available.

Do you offer digital marketing consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations for businesses interested in digital marketing strategies. Please schedule a consultation through our website or contact our business development team.

How can I partner with Digits Myanmar?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, businesses, and industry organizations. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@digitsmyanmar.com to discuss opportunities.
