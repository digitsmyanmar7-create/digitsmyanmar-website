Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Digits Myanmar

How can I join the Digits Myanmar community?

You can join our community by signing up on our website, attending our monthly meetups, or participating in our online forums. Membership is free for all digital marketing enthusiasts in Myanmar.

Are your digital marketing courses free?

Yes! All our basic digital marketing courses and resources are completely free. We also offer premium workshops and certification programs for more advanced training at affordable rates.

How do I access your digital marketing tools?

After registering on our website, you'll get access to our free tools section. For premium tools, you can subscribe to our Pro membership which gives you full access to all our advanced digital marketing tools and resources.

Do you offer certification for digital marketing skills?

Yes, we offer recognized certifications for various digital marketing specializations including SEO, Social Media Marketing, Content Strategy, and Analytics. Our certification programs include both theoretical knowledge and practical assessments.

Can I contribute content or tutorials to Digits Myanmar?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from experienced digital marketers. You can submit your content through our contributor portal, and our editorial team will review it for publication on our platform.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

You can report any technical issues through our support ticket system or by emailing tech-support@digitsmyanmar.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue, including screenshots if applicable.
