About Digits Myanmar

Driving Myanmar's Digital Marketing Revolution Through Education, Community, and Innovation

Our Story

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to Myanmar's digital marketing community

Our Mission

To democratize digital marketing knowledge in Myanmar by providing accessible education, fostering industry collaboration, and empowering professionals and businesses to thrive in the digital economy. We bridge the skills gap through comprehensive resources, hands-on training, and community support.

Our Vision

To establish Myanmar as a digital marketing hub in Southeast Asia by 2030, where every marketer has access to world-class education and resources. We envision a vibrant ecosystem where digital innovation drives economic growth and creates sustainable opportunities for all.

Our Story

How Digits Myanmar began and our journey so far

From Humble Beginnings to National Impact

Digits Myanmar was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate digital marketers who recognized the growing need for structured digital marketing education in Myanmar. What started as a small community of 20 professionals sharing knowledge has grown into Myanmar's premier digital marketing association with thousands of members nationwide.

During the digital transformation accelerated by global events, we identified critical gaps in digital skills and resources available to Myanmar businesses and professionals. This realization fueled our commitment to building a comprehensive platform that addresses these needs through education, community building, and industry advocacy.

Today, we partner with leading industry players, educational institutions, and government bodies to shape the future of digital marketing in Myanmar. Our journey continues as we expand our reach and deepen our impact across all regions of the country.
